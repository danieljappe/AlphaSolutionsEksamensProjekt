About BroadWave

Welcome to BroadWave, the ultimate project management tool that's here to give Trello a run for its money!

With BroadWave, you can easily create boards, assign tasks, and track progress - all in one sleek and intuitive interface. Plus, our customization options let you create custom labels and filter tasks by due date, priority, or any other criteria you can dream up. Our shit so fire!

So whether you're managing a team or flying solo, BroadWave is the perfect tool to help you stay organized and on track. Sign up today and experience the future of project management!

Fun fact: BroadWave was created as a computer science project but ended up being the best product on the market outcompeting big sites like Trello.
